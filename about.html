Marie used to walk home from school and stop by the market place. She would smell the pate in the stands and would rush home to beg her mom for money. When her mom did giv her money, she would walk to the pate stands and buy one. Walking back home, she would find herself wanting more pate. And each time she went back, they would all be sold out. As she moved to America, she found herself wanting to taste another pate, but many places did not make her want to go back for seconds.
